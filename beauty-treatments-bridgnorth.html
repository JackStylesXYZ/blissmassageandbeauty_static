Beauty Treatments in Bridgnorth

Professional beauty treatments in a calm, unhurried setting. Based in Bridgnorth, Shropshire, each treatment is delivered with care and attention to detail, helping you feel polished, refreshed and confident.

From waxing and tinting to hands and feet therapies, all treatments are carried out in a private, comfortable environment where you can relax and feel at ease.

Waxing

Professional waxing services for smooth, long-lasting results. Whether you're preparing for a holiday, a special occasion or simply prefer the convenience of waxing, all treatments are carried out with care and attention to your comfort.

A range of waxing options are available including eyebrow shaping, lip and chin, underarm, arms, bikini line, legs and more. Warm wax is used for a thorough, comfortable finish.

Who it's for: Anyone seeking hair removal with longer-lasting results than shaving, or preparing for holidays and special occasions.

Benefits: Smooth skin for weeks, reduced regrowth over time, precise shaping and a polished finish.

Please note: A patch test is required 48 hours before your first tinting treatment if you are having eyebrow or eyelash tinting alongside waxing.

To book waxing, call or WhatsApp to arrange your appointment.

Tinting

Eyebrow and eyelash tinting adds definition and depth to your natural features, creating a polished look that lasts for weeks. It's ideal for those who prefer a low-maintenance beauty routine or want to enhance their brows and lashes without daily makeup.

Tinting is also popular before holidays, as it means you can wake up looking naturally defined without the need for mascara or brow pencils. The process is gentle, quick and tailored to suit your colouring.

Who it's for: Anyone wanting natural definition, preparing for holidays, or those with lighter brows and lashes who prefer a fuller look.

Benefits: Long-lasting definition, low-maintenance beauty routine, natural-looking enhancement and water-resistant results.

Important: A patch test is required 48 hours prior to your first tinting treatment. This is a quick, simple process to ensure the tint is suitable for you.

To arrange a patch test or book tinting, please get in touch or message on WhatsApp.

Hands & Feet

Manicures, pedicures and shellac treatments to keep your hands and feet looking well-cared for and polished. Whether you're after classic nail care, a shellac finish that lasts, or simply want to give tired feet some attention, all treatments are delivered with care and patience.

Manicures and pedicures include nail shaping, cuticle care, exfoliation and massage, followed by polish or shellac application. Shellac offers a chip-resistant, high-shine finish that lasts up to two weeks, making it ideal for holidays or busy lifestyles.

Who it's for: Anyone wanting polished nails, preparing for events or holidays, or those seeking long-lasting nail colour with shellac.

Benefits: Well-groomed hands and feet, long-lasting colour, relaxation and a polished, confident finish.

Treatments available: Classic manicure and pedicure, shellac manicure and pedicure, shellac removal, and removal with reapplication.

To book a manicure or pedicure, call or send a WhatsApp message.
