Holistic Therapies in Bridgnorth

Holistic therapies work gently to support balance, relaxation and wellbeing. Based in Bridgnorth, Shropshire, each treatment is designed to work with your body's natural ability to restore itself, helping you feel calmer, more grounded and supported.

Whether you're seeking relief from stress, support during a difficult time, or simply a moment of deep rest, these therapies offer a gentle and nurturing approach to self-care.

Reflexology

Reflexology is a gentle therapy that applies pressure to specific points on the feet, which correspond to different areas of the body. It encourages relaxation, supports circulation and helps the body restore its natural balance.

This treatment is particularly calming and can be helpful for those experiencing stress, sleep difficulties or digestive discomfort. You remain fully clothed throughout, and the session takes place in a quiet, supportive environment.

Who it's for: Anyone seeking deep relaxation, stress relief, improved sleep, or support with digestive wellbeing.

Benefits: Promotes relaxation, supports circulation, encourages restful sleep, eases tension and helps restore balance naturally.

To book reflexology, call or WhatsApp today.

Reiki

Reiki is a deeply relaxing energy therapy that supports balance and healing on a physical, emotional and mental level. It works gently and intuitively, helping to ease stress, restore calm and promote a sense of peace.

During a Reiki session, you remain fully clothed while gentle energy work is offered through light touch or hands held just above the body. Many people find the experience deeply restful, comforting and grounding.

Who it's for: Those experiencing stress, emotional difficulty, sleep disruption, or anyone seeking a gentle, non-invasive treatment for wellbeing.

Benefits: Eases stress and anxiety, promotes deep relaxation, supports emotional balance, encourages restful sleep and aids natural healing.

If you'd like to experience Reiki, please get in touch or message on WhatsApp.

Holistic Facial

A holistic facial combines gentle skincare with relaxation techniques to nourish the skin and ease tension held in the face, neck and shoulders. It's a calming treatment that supports both outer radiance and inner calm.

Using natural products suited to your skin, the treatment includes cleansing, gentle exfoliation, massage and a soothing mask. The focus is on relaxation as much as skincare, leaving you feeling refreshed and restored.

Who it's for: Anyone seeking gentle skincare, facial relaxation, or a treatment that combines beauty and wellbeing.

Benefits: Nourishes and refreshes the skin, releases facial tension, promotes relaxation, supports natural radiance and provides a nurturing self-care experience.

To book a holistic facial, call or send a WhatsApp message.

Ear Candles

Ear candling is a traditional therapy that uses hollow candles to create a gentle warmth and soothing effect. It's often chosen by those experiencing sinus congestion, ear pressure or headaches, and is known for its deeply relaxing qualities.

The treatment is gentle, calming and non-invasive. While you relax, the candles are carefully placed and lit, creating a soft crackling sound and a warming sensation that many find deeply comforting.

Who it's for: Those experiencing sinus congestion, ear discomfort, headaches, or anyone seeking a gentle, calming treatment.

Benefits: Eases sinus pressure, supports ear comfort, promotes relaxation, may ease headaches and provides a calming sensory experience.

To book ear candling, call or WhatsApp to arrange your appointment.
