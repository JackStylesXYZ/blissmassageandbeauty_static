Massage in Bridgnorth

Whether you're seeking relief from muscle tension, support during pregnancy, or simply a moment to unwind, massage therapy offers a gentle and effective way to restore comfort and balance. Based in Bridgnorth, Shropshire, each treatment is tailored to your needs in a calm, private setting.

Swedish Massage

Swedish massage is a classic relaxation treatment that uses flowing, rhythmic strokes to ease tension and promote a sense of deep calm. It's gentle, soothing and ideal for anyone new to massage or looking to simply switch off.

This treatment focuses on improving circulation, releasing surface tension and encouraging the body to relax naturally. The pressure is moderate and can be adjusted to suit your comfort throughout the session.

Who it's for: Anyone seeking stress relief, improved sleep, general relaxation, or a gentle introduction to massage therapy.

Benefits: Reduces muscle tension, improves circulation, supports relaxation, eases stress and promotes restful sleep.

To book your Swedish massage, call or WhatsApp today.

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage works on the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue to release chronic tension, knots and areas of discomfort. It uses slower, more focused pressure to reach the root of tension and support long-term relief.

This treatment is particularly helpful for those who experience persistent tightness, postural strain or discomfort from physical activity. While firm, the pressure is always guided by your feedback and comfort level.

Who it's for: Those with ongoing muscle tension, sports-related discomfort, postural strain, or anyone who prefers deeper pressure.

Benefits: Releases chronic tension, improves mobility, supports recovery from strain, and helps ease long-standing discomfort.

Ready to ease tension? Get in touch or message on WhatsApp.

Pregnancy Massage

Pregnancy massage is a nurturing treatment designed to support comfort and wellbeing during pregnancy. Using gentle techniques and supportive positioning, it helps ease the physical demands of pregnancy in a safe, relaxing environment.

This massage can help relieve lower back discomfort, reduce swelling, ease tired legs and encourage restful sleep. It's adapted to each stage of pregnancy and focuses on areas where tension and discomfort are most common.

Who it's for: Expectant mothers after the first trimester who are seeking relief from pregnancy-related discomfort.

Benefits: Eases back and hip discomfort, reduces swelling, promotes relaxation, supports better sleep and provides a nurturing moment of self-care.

If you'd like to book a pregnancy massage, please call or WhatsApp to discuss your needs.

Indian Head Massage

Indian head massage is a relaxing treatment that focuses on the upper back, shoulders, neck, scalp and face. It's ideal for relieving tension headaches, mental fatigue and stress that accumulates in the head and shoulders.

This treatment is performed while you're seated and fully clothed, making it a convenient option for a shorter session. It uses a combination of gentle pressure, kneading and rhythmic movements to release tightness and restore calm.

Who it's for: Anyone experiencing tension headaches, shoulder stiffness, mental fatigue, or those seeking a shorter, focused treatment.

Benefits: Relieves headaches, eases shoulder and neck tension, promotes mental clarity, reduces stress and supports relaxation.

To book your Indian head massage, call or send a message.
